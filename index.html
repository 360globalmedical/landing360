Our matching engine scores 670+ suppliers against each request using 4 criteria: product category fit, regulatory licence, geographic reach, and historical response rate — proposing the best 10–15 in milliseconds.

670+  supplier profiles continuously updated
1-Click Outreach

Personalised emails at scale

A single click generates and sends personalised, professional RFQ emails to every selected supplier — each tailored to their communication style, language, and prior relationship history.

Quote Analysis

Best option scored automatically

When supplier quotes arrive, our AI reads and extracts price, available quantity, expiry date, lead time, and documentation status — then scores every option with a weighted algorithm to surface the optimal choice.
